# Workflow

Different organizations/teams/projects/products might have different workflows or SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) for their virtual engineering team. The workflow can be customized based on the specific needs and requirements of the project.

Document the workflow in either text, flowchart, or diagram format. Automate it.

Though this might involved some organization wide workflow or confidential information, it is still a good idea to document the workflow in a way that can be shared with the virtual engineering team. This will help to ensure that the virtual engineers are following the same workflow and SOPs, and that they are aware of the expectations and requirements of the project.
